I am also lecturer at the École Normale Supérieure de Paris where I supervise the exercice sessions of the dynamical systems course given by Raphaël Cerf. Starting from October 2022, I will be a Herchel Smith Research Fellow at the DPMMS (University of Cambridge). A version of my thesis can be found here. Here is my CV.

Research interests

I study certain zeta functions associated to hyperbolic dynamical systems (geodesic flows in negative curvature, billiard flows) and their link with the underlying topology ; those functions are studied with the help of functionnal and microlocal analytic tools, through the prism of Pollicott-Ruelle resonances.
