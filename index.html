Research interests

I work in dynamical systems, microlocal analysis, and differential geometry. My research focuses on the interplay between geometry, dynamics, and spectral theory, especially in the study of hyperbolic flows, zeta functions, and Pollicott–Ruelle resonances.

More recently, I have also been interested in kinetic equations in chaotic settings, statistical inference for spectral problems, and geometric Lévy processes.
