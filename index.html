Yann Chaubet   


I am assistant professor (maître de conférences) at the LMJL (Nantes Université). Before that, I was Herchel Smith Research Fellow at the DPMMS (Cambridge University). My Ph.D thesis was defended at the LMO (Université Paris-Saclay) and was supervised by Colin Guillarmou. A version of my thesis can be found here. Here is my CV.

Research interests

I study certain zeta functions associated to hyperbolic dynamical systems (geodesic flows in negative curvature, billiard flows) and their link with the underlying topology ; those functions are studied with the help of functionnal and microlocal analytic tools, through the prism of Pollicott-Ruelle resonances.
